Handpoke Tattoos in Lisbon

Hi, I'm Anna. I'm a handpoke tattoo artist,
currently living in Lisbon, Portugal.

Handpoke is a technique where no machine is used. You only need a needle and tattoo master's hands. It is a comeback to the roots, it's something original, it's how it was at the beginning. It requires full concentration on the process. Dot by dot we can create something unique and meaningful.

About me

I've been doing handpoke tattoos for more than 2 years now. I enjoy bringing to life different designs. I'm especially inspired by nature and by Slavic and Berber ornaments. I believe there is a meaning Behind Every Tattoo. But you decide what the meaning would be.
